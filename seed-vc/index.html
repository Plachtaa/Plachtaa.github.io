Seed-VC

Seed-VC is named after Seed-TTS as the method and training scheme is inspired by the paper's description about voice conversion.

We may change name based on future development of this project.

This page is for showing results and comparisons only. The examples of other VC models are directly retrieved from their demo pages.

Zero-shot Voice Conversion

We present our results of zero-shot voice conversion comparing to other previous released/published voice conversion models.
